Social magnet

Medical social media, no white noise

Social networks including Facebook and Twitter thrive on pushing information to users based on their age, demographics and activity online.

This can be positive, but your personal browsing habits can often conflict with your professional financial profile, resulting in content that might be right for you, but not your clients and prospective customers.

MarketAIR eliminates the white noise and provides only social posts from high quality, pre-screened pages, relevant to your specialist financial area. Share, save or auto-schedule.

News magnet

Filtered medical news. Personalised.

MarketAIR’s intelligence systems crawl are constantly crawling the internet, searching for high quality journalism, specific to financial professionals and investors alike.

You can view, save, share and auto-schedule any content with the push of a button.

Research magnet

Save it for later viewing, share it online with clients and investors or use it as a foundation for a financial update on Facebook or Twitter.
